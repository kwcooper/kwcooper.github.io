I am a Masters student in the Industrial and Labor Relations Program at Cornell University persuing a carreer to bridge the gap between psychology research in the lab and our everyday lives.

Experience

Research Intern

Centerstone Research Institute

Worked with the data analysis of community based research projects related to the implementation of clinical psychology techniques in individual psychotherapy. Identified potential eligible clients for studies based on a set of inclusion criteria, scheduled surveys for clients, participated in client interaction through administering surveys over the phone, and aggregated data to create data packets for implementation teams.

August 2017 - January 2018

Research Assistant

Newman Memory Lab

Led and ran research projects utilizing in-vivo electrophysiological recordings coupled with study specific behavioral tasks. Supervised and trained graduate and undergraduate researchers in laboratory techniques. Constructed handmade micro-electrode drives for in-vivo electrophysiological recordings, modifying design as needed to target different brain regions for different experiments. Performed aseptic surgery to implant electrodes into the brain, analyzed the resulting electrophysiology, and delivered lab wide presentation of research findings.

Dec 2015 - Present
